Turn Queue
  1. The goal is to be the last spaceship standing.
  2. You can defeat your opponents by depleting their healthbars or by dropping them off of the map
  3. Press 'A' or 'D' to move left or right
  4. There is a limit to how far you can move per turn
  5. Press 'W' or 'S' to adjust the angle of your shot
  6. Hold and let go of 'Space' to choose the power for your shot
  7. Click '1', '2', or '3' on the weapon selector to choose your weapon
  8. Depending on your weapon choice, you will experience a delay for your turns
    1. Weapon 1 adds a delay of 100
    2. Weapon 2 adds a delay of 300
    3. Weapon 3 adds a delay of 600 and this weapon needs 3 turns to recharge
  9. Watch out for changes in gravitational force and direction changes shown by the compass
