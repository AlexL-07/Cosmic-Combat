Turn Queue
  1. The goal is to be the last spaceship standing.
  2. You can defeat your opponents by depleting their healthbars or dropping them off of the map.
  3. Press 'A' or 'D' to move left or right
  4. Press 'W' or 'S' to change the angle of your shot
  5. Press and hold 'Space' to choose the power
  6. Click '1', '2', or '3' on the weapon selector to choose your weapon.
  7. Depending on your weapon choice, you will experience a delay for your turns.
  8. Watch out for gravity changes.
